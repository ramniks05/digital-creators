## Recommendation
For Digital Creatorss (Software Development & Digital Marketing), I recommend:

**Option 1: Teal/Indigo** - Modern, tech-forward, professional
OR
**Option 7: Cyan/Blue** - Fresh, modern, approachable

Both work well for software development companies and convey innovation and professionalism.
